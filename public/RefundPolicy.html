Cancellation and Refund Policy

This policy outlines the procedure for cancelling orders and seeking refunds for products or services purchased through the Platform. Under this policy:

1. Cancellations:

  • Cancellation requests will only be considered if submitted within 7 working days of placing the order. However, requests may not be entertained if orders have already been communicated to sellers or merchants listed on the Platform and they have initiated shipping, or if the product is out for delivery. In such cases, customers may opt to reject the product at the doorstep.
  • Travelauncher Solutions Private Limited does not accept cancellation requests for perishable items such as flowers, food items, undergarments or fragrances. However, refunds or replacements can be processed if the user establishes that the delivered product's quality is unsatisfactory.

2. Damaged or Defective Items:

  • If you receive damaged or defective items, please promptly report the issue to our customer service team. Requests will be processed once the seller or merchant listed on the Platform has verified and confirmed the issue. This should be reported within 7 days of receiving the products.
  • If the product received does not match the description on the site or your expectations, notify our customer service team within 7 days of receipt. Our team will investigate your complaint and make an appropriate decision.

3. Warranty-Related Complaints:

  • For products covered under manufacturer warranties, please direct your complaints to the respective manufacturers.

4. Refunds:

  • Any approved refunds by Travelauncher Solutions Private Limited will be processed within 30 days.

This policy governs all cancellations and refund requests made through the Platform.
